About SecureX

SecureX is a simple, secure, and local password manager built to help you store and manage your login credentials right in your browser. No cloud, no signup, just quick access to your passwords.

With SecureX, you can add, view, copy, and delete passwords easily. We prioritize simplicity and speed so you stay focused on what matters.

This tool is perfect for anyone who wants a lightweight password management solution without complexity or accounts.
